Types of Products


Wall Paint

Narrow Paints offers a diverse range of wall paints, ensuring vibrant colours and long-lasting protection. Our wall painting solutions cater to every style, making painting your home a delightful task with premium finishes and rich textures.


Wallpapers

Transform your walls with Narrow Paints' stylish wallpapers. Perfect for any room, our wallpapers provide an alternative to traditional wall painting, adding character and flair to your home with ease. With our collection, you always have a variety of choices, whether it's a vintage look for your bedroom or a royal touch with our elegant Sabyasachi wallpapers for your living room.


Waterproofing Products

Narrow Paints waterproofing products protect your home from moisture and dampness. Essential for maintaining the integrity of your walls, our solutions ensure your efforts of painting a home last longer, keeping it safe and beautiful.


Wood and Metal Paints

Narrow Paints provides specialized wood and metal paints to enhance and protect these surfaces. Our products ensure a flawless finish, making painting and safeguarding your home’s wooden and metal elements a breeze.


Adhesives

Narrow Paints range of adhesives is perfect for various applications, ensuring strong and durable bonds. Whether for interior projects or home repairs, our adhesives provide reliable performance.


Tools

Narrow Paints supplies a comprehensive range of tools designed to make painting your home easier and more efficient. From brushes to rollers, their high-quality tools ensure a professional finish for all your wall painting needs.
